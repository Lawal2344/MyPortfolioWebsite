My Process

How I turn ideas
into great products

A structured, research-backed design process that ensures every product I build is both beautiful and effective.

01
🔍
Discover
Deep research into user needs, business goals, and market landscape. I interview users, audit competitors, and define the core problem to solve.
02
💡
Define
Synthesizing research into clear insights. User personas, journey maps, and a clear design brief that aligns stakeholders before a single pixel is drawn.
03
✏️
Design
From rough wireframes to high-fidelity Figma prototypes. Iterating quickly, testing early, and crafting a visual language that resonates with users.
04
🚀
Deliver
Developer-ready design specs, detailed component libraries, and full handoff documentation. I stay involved through launch to ensure pixel-perfect implementation.
